My publications focus on underwater networking, secure and trustworthy communications, intelligent routing and data collection, underwater perception, and learning-enabled communication systems. For the latest citation counts and updates, please visit my Google Scholar profile.

Author notation: R. Zhu denotes Rongxin Zhu; * denotes corresponding author.
