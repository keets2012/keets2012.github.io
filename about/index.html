Biography

Dr. Rongxin Zhu received his Ph.D. in Cyberspace Security in 2024. He is currently a lecturer at Hainan University and an adjunct research fellow at the Paradise Lab, University of Ottawa.

His research focuses on underwater networks and communications, secure and trustworthy networking, embodied and semantic communications, and AI-enabled underwater perception.
