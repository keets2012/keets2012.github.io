About Rongxin ZHU

Rongxin ZHU

Dr. Rongxin Zhu received his Ph.D. in Cyberspace Security in 2024. He is currently an adjunct research fellow at Paradise Lab, University of Ottawa, and a lecturer at Hainan University. Dr. Zhu was a visiting scholar at the University of Ottawa for one year. His research interests focus on the security of underwater sensor networks and the development of intelligent algorithms.
